The ingredients needed to make Dry Chilli chicken:
  1. Provide 250 gm boneless chicken
  2. Provide 1 cup cubed bell paper any Colors
  3. Get 1 cup cubed onion
  4. Prepare 1 Tsp crushed garlic
  5. Take 4 Tsp +2 tsp extra cornflour
  6. Take 1 Tsp chilli flakes
  7. You need 1 Tsp soya sauce
  8. Take 1 Tsp veniger
  9. Get To taste Salt
  10. You need as needed Oil for frying
  11. You need 1 Tsp Green chilli chopped
Instructions to make Dry Chilli chicken:
  1. Wash & wipe the chicken.keep it in a plate or bowl.
  2. Add cornflor,1/2 Tsp soya sauce,salt,1/2 garlic paste.
  3. Spread it on the chicken.
  4. In a Heat pan add oil let it hot.
  5. Put chicken pieces & Fry till crispy.
  6. Remove it from the flame.
  7. Take another pan,add 2 Tsp oil,let it hot.
  8. Add garlic,saute it.add onion & Capsicum saute it.
  9. Now add sauce, veniger, chilli flax,salt,mix it.add 1/2 cup of water.
  10. In a small bowl add 2 Tsp cornflour,add 4 tsp water.mix it very well.pour it into the boiling mixtures.let it boil 1 miniute.
  11. Add fried chicken pieces, sprinkle origeno it.
  12. Let it some dry.
  13. Serve it hot - hot in a bowl.
  14. It can be take like a snacks,or side dish.
