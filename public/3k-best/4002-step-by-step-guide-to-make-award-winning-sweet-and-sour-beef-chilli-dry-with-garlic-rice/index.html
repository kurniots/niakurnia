The ingredients needed to make Sweet And Sour Beef Chilli Dry With Garlic Rice:
  1. You need For batter :
  2. Provide 1 egg
  3. You need 4 tbsp corn flour
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp Soya sauce
  5. Use 1 tsp salt
  6. You need 1/2 tsp White pepper pwd
  7. Provide For beef chilli :
  8. Prepare Beef undercut or beef strips 1/2 kg
  9. Provide to taste Salt
  10. You need 1 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  11. Take Chicken cubes 1
  12. Provide Onion sliced 1 big
  13. Prepare Green chili slices 8
  14. Provide 1 cup Spring onion
  15. Get 2 Potato for kids (optional)
  16. Use 1 cup Oil
  17. Get 1/4 cup Garlic chopped
  18. Provide 1 tbsp Sugar
  19. You need 2 tsp Black pepper pwd
  20. Provide 1 tsp White pepper pwd
  21. Take 1 tbsp Soya sauce
  22. Use 1 tbsp Vinegar
  23. You need 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  24. Provide 1 tbsp Chilli sauce
  25. Prepare For Rice :
  26. Provide Garlic chopped
  27. Get to taste Salt
  28. Use 1/4 cup Oil
  29. Provide 1 tbsp Soya sauce
  30. Provide 2 tsp Black pepper pwd
  31. Provide 1 tbsp Vinegar
  32. Take 2 cups Rice
  33. Take Water as required
Steps to make Sweet And Sour Beef Chilli Dry With Garlic Rice:
  1. Beat egg add salt, pepper, corn flour, soya sauce and make better.
  2. If you use undercut you don't have to boil meat, you can directly Deep in batter and fry. But I use beef thigh strips so I boiled with salt, gg paste, chicken cubes. When its done cool completely, add them in batter and deep fry them.
  3. I used potatoes for kids it's totally optional, but it's taste good with beef chilli. Cut potatoes in cubes and fry in sane oil.
  4. Now heat some oil add garlic chopped, fry well, now add, soya sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, salt(u can adjust salt according to your taste), sugar,black pepper pwd and cook for awhile.
  5. Cut sliced onions, green chili, spring onions.
  6. Add beef, all the veggies and mix well, serve immediately.
  7. For Rice : heat oil add garlic chopped, now add salt, black pepper, soya sauce, vinegar. Add your rice, water cook until water dries, put your rice on dum for about 10 mins.
